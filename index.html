SWEET MENU ITEMS 

  • Flavor Filled Fortune Cookies
    Fortune! Fortune! Get your fortunes here! Try to special flavored fortune cookies. You could get strawberry, vanilla, or chocolate filled fortune cookies, but it's always a surprise. How fun! You get one free with every meal. Additional cookies are $1 each 
  • Japanese Butter Cookies
    Who doesn't love cookies? These Japanese butter cookies are made from butter (of course), flour, egg and a large helping of sugar. They are the perfect combination of crunch, simplicity, and sweetness, I could eat them all day. $2.00 each.
  • Sweet Rice Cakes
    Also called Mochi, these rice cakes are made from japonica rice, water, sugar, and corn starch. It's hard to believe rice could be so sweet! With an assortmet of flavors ranging from vanilla to peanut butter to berry, you won't be disappointed. Mochi pieces are $3 each.  
  • Black Sesame Cookies
    These addicting cookies are crunchy, crisp, sweet, nutty, and just all around delicious. Who knew that sesame seeds could taste so good? Sesame seeds are one of the most popular ingredients in Japanese baking. We hope that you'll like it too! Each cookie is $4
